# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Showing empathy and respect.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Trolling or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Open a private maintainer contact through GitHub if an incident needs review.
